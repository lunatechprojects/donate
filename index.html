Our Stewardship Promise

Our objective is to assure that every dollar you donate is used to help as many people as possible! Gifts that are designated for a particular appeal will be used as requested until that need has been fully funded. Any remaining funds are put to use where it is needed most. If a project cannot be completed for reasons beyond our control, the board reserves the right the use such funds for other similar projects. We will never share your contact information with anyone.

Donation Policies:

  • Currency - All online donations to Blazing Faith Ministries are processed in Canadian funds. We deposit cheques and money orders made in US currency into our Canadian bank US currency account.
  • Tax Receipts - All donations made to Blazing Faith Ministries will receive an official donation tax receipt (in Canadian dollar value) at the end of each calendar year
  • Security - We have state-of-the-art security networks in place for your online donations. Your information is safe!
  • Refunds - No refunds will be made once the donation has been processed.
